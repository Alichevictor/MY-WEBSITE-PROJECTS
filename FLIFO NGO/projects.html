Our Projects

Empowerment

FLIFO engage in empowerment projects for the VULNERABLE DOWN-TRODDENS in the society, so the poor can still be skilled and be well versed with all the technological complexities and options for good livelihood.

Education

We believe that the purpose of education is to make the child a self-motivated and independent learner. FLIFO strives to develop educational opportunities for all children based on this idea. FLIFO conducts sensitization programs for awaking people about importance of education through the following activities: 1. Community Mobilization and Awareness Programs 2. School materials procurement and distributions to school children

Agricultural Program

Farming has always been one of the most important economic activities in NIGERIA and AFRICA at large. The large Nigerian population largely engage in rural farming and depent on it as means of livelihood.

Skills Developement

Majority of NIGERIA's population still live in the villages. Large number of youths are also increasing in the villages. The number of youth is increasing faster than the rate of job creation. Youths are considered as the future of any nation.The youth comprises over one-third of the NIGERIA population which in turn constitutes a major part of the labour force of the country. The number of underprivileged youth in NIGERIA who lack education and proper guidance is tremendous.

Child an Social Welfare

FLIFO aims towards lasting improvements in the quality of life of disadvantage and marginalised children living in abject poverty with focus towards protection and promotion of the rights, interests and dignity of children. Our approach to child development seeks to simultaneously address to physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects of a child’s life. Thus all child development programmes incorporate a variety of approaches to help educate and grow children into healthy sustainable adults

Old Age Welfare

We work for old age society as we know that majority of of the elderly population lives in rural Nigeria. Factors like literacy level, economic independence, age-old dependency ratio, diseases and other health impacts vary. With rapid urbanisation and a shift in socio-economic priorities, traditional family structures and values have changed. Meager old age pensions provided by the government of NIGERIA are insufficient.

Free Medical Checkup

Due to lack of hygienic awareness, gender issues, superstitions, etc., several health related problems exist in the working area including higher mortality.Hence to improve health conditions, FLIFO promoted community meetings and other sensitization programs, health check up awareness camps. .
